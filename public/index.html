Platform Research

The HealthAI platform is an AI-powered conversational assistant designed to provide first-level health screening to individuals who may otherwise delay seeking medical help due to social stigma or limited access. It identifies mental health concerns and early physical disease risks through simple, structured conversations.

Scientifically grounded in SDG 3, the platform utilizes an Extra Trees Classifier model to achieve 81.8% accuracy in risk classification. This foundation allows for the non-invasive analysis of complex symptom patterns often overlooked in traditional healthcare settings.
